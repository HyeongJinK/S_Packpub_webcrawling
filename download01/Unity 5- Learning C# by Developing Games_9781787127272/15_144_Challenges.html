Challenges

For those of you who want to do more with this project, there are plenty of things you can do, especially after finishing the rest of this book. Here are some ideas to get you thinking:

  • Add a Credits screen to your main menu

  • Add in three color sliders to customize your ship's color (renderer.material.color) with red, green, and blue values

  • Add in Music Volume and SFX Volume sliders, and use those values to set the volume of sounds you play!

  • Modify the style of the buttons to reflect your own game!
