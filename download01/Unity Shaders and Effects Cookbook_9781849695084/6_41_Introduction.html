Introduction

Transparency and graphics cards can be a little tricky at first when writing Shaders. By utilizing Unity's Surface Shaders we can quite easily start to build Shaders that can have full transparency for surfaces like glass, or partial transparency for surfaces like hair and foliage.

We are going to look at how to first construct a very simple Shader using transparency, build our knowledge set to include Shaders such as hair, and understand how transparency affects the order in which your objects are drawn.
