The result

We can now press play and test the agile ninja, our endless runner game. We should see the background scrolling, the dust particles emitting from behind the ninja's feet, and an obstacle randomly spawning repeatedly. To avoid the obstacles, we need to time the ninja's jump to go over obstacles and his slide to go under the obstacles.

We need to hold down the W key to perform a full jump, and likewise, we hold down the S key for as long as we want to slide.
