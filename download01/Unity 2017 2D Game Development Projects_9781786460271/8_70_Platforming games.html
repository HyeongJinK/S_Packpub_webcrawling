Platforming games

Unlike collecting and shooting games, platformers require a bit more dexterity when it comes to creating gameplay and the player traversing the game's levels. Similar to the other game genres that we have explored, platforming games also have several variations. There are the more common ones like Sonic and Mario. In their essence, platforming games require you to traverse across platforms to navigate around the environment.
