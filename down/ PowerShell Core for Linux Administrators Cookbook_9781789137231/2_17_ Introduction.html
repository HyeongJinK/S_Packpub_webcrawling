Introduction

It is a common notion that the more you use the Terminal (as opposed to the GUI), the more efficient you are. Typing out commands is much easier and faster than clicking around the screen. However, to someone who has just begun using the Terminal, it may not be so. Over time, as administrators grow more and more comfortable with the Terminal, they learn to configure it for speed and efficiency, much like training a horse.
