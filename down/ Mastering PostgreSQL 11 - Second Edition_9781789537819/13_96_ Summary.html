Summary

In this chapter, we learned how to move to PostgreSQL. Migration is an important topic, and more and more people are adopting PostgreSQL as we speak.

PostgreSQL 11 has many new features such as improved built-in partitioning, and a lot more. In the future, we will see many more developments in all areas of PostgreSQL, especially to allow users to scale out more and to run queries even faster. We are yet to see what the future has in store for us.
