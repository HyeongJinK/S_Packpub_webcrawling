Summary

In this chapter, we learned about the most important features of PostgreSQL replication, such as streaming replication and replication conflicts. We then learned about PITR, as well as replication slots. Note that a book on replication is never complete unless it spans around 400 pages or so, but we have learned the most important things that every administrator should know.

In Chapter 11, Deciding on Useful Extensions, will be about useful extensions to PostgreSQL. We will learn about extensions that have been widely adopted by the industry and that provide even more functionality.
