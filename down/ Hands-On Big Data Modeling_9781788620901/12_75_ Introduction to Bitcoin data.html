Introduction to Bitcoin data

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency that is currently very popular. According to Wikipedia:

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or a single administrator and can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer Bitcoin network directly, without the need for intermediaries.

There is lots of data related to Bitcoin. In this exercise, we are going to use the dataset provided by Kaggle[4]. We are going to use this public dataset to predict the future price of Bitcoin.
