Summary

Twitter is one of the most important social media tools used in today's world. It provides a platform to share people's opinions, facts, and information regarding people, places, animals, and things. These tweets are used by private, governmental, and non-governmental organizations to mine different types of information, including business intelligence. In this chapter, we learned how to use Twitter data to generate several business-intelligent models. 

In the next chapter, we are going to use the IMDB dataset in Python to generate several models related to movies, ratings, and views.
