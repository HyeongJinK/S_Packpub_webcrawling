Summary

Managing big data requires distinct methods compared to traditional DBMS. There are many Big Data Management Systems that can facilitate in this process. Most popular one includes Redix, AsterixDB, Aerospike, and others. In this chapter, we discussed some of the characteristics of BDMS and explored the usage of Redix, AsterixDB for storing semi-unstructured data.
