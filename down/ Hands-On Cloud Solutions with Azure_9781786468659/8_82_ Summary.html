Summary

There is no magic bullet for deploying solutions in Azure. Throughout this chapter, we have looked at some dos and don'ts for a few of the resources in Azure. I hope my experiences have been helpful. Remember, your journey with Azure is a marathon, not a sprint—fail fast and learn faster. Work at using a DevOps approach to Azure, and ensure you put yourself in a good position to support your resources. 

Innovation hinges on the backs of the courageous, those that challenge the every day, and feel they can do it better. So, don't fear the new—get to know it.
